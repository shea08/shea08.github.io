# About

Ben Shea is a husband, low-power radio enthusiast, DIY technologist, and bass guitar player. He is currently transitioning into a career in healthcare and currently works as a Certified Nurse Assistant. His problem-solving skills and expertise have been used to improve the efficiency and continuous improvement of many business processes.

Ben received his B.S in Biology from Southeast Missouri State University. While serving in the United States Air Force as a <a href="https://www.22af.afrc.af.mil/News/Article-Display/Article/614112/45-years-on-alert-minot-conducts-minuteman-iii-test-launch/">Nuclear Operations Officer</a>, he earned his M.S. in Occupational Health and Safety from Indiana State University.
